DAY #1 GET YOUR FIRST BOX OF PLANT-BASED MILK

The best way to start your 12 day cow-milk free experience is to go to the store and find yourself a 1l of plant based milk. What are your options? Most popular vegan milks are made of soy, oats, almond and coconut and they would be our recommendation for your first plant-based milk experience. Denmark is an oasis of life and prosperity that takes care of its people. We have taxes, though we do see the results in infrastructure and healthcare, we go through the hardships of higher education though we get paid for it, we are fired by Odin knows what reason though the state takes care of us through financial aid. We are being taken care of so well though how much are we taking care of the other beings we share the land with?

The Danish diet contains a high proportion of animal products which gets us to producing cruelty with slaughtering more than twice the global average a year. But we are not going to take that path. It is quite easy to realize that, as an organization, one would try to get to you through facts, statistics and very very long and boring how to’s and why’s but the game has changed. We try to keep that low on this page and to bring you the experience of a different type of week through a proposal.
